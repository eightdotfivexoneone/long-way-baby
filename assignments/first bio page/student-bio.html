Student Bio

Steve Bowden

Steve received his undergraduate degree from Maine College of Art in 1997, co-founded the New Media department of Benetton's Fabrica in 1998, received his masters degree at Cranbrook Academy in 2005 and awarded a research residency at the Jan Van Eyck Academy in 2013. He is currently a professor of design, typography and new media.
